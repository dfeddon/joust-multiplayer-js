RED vs BLUE

Defend your teammates and crush your opponents in the Wingdom's thrilling PvP tournament that pits 20 Red Team players against 20 Blue Team players!


CAPTURE THE FLAGS

Earn critical team bonuses by siezing more territory than your opponent. Territory is defined by 3 flags:

  Team flags begin the game at their respective home base. Steal your opponents' flag to win more territory!

  The neutural white flag is housed at the map's center. Push this flag into enemy space to gain more bonus points!

  Numbered plaques are spread throughout the map, ordered from left to right. Plant flags into them sequentially to advance your territory one plaque at a time!


BUFFS & BONUSES

While you begin a hapless wingling, over time your power will grow. Earn progression bonuses, buffs and slot unlocks as you level up and survive wave after perilous wave of assault!

  Yellow potions grant temporary bonus increases, while health boosts are awarded by green potions. The healthier you are, the faster you fly!

  Found in chests, buffs provide temporary boons and abilities such as hitting harder, speed boosts, invisibility, protective bubbles, and much, much more!

  Level up from levels 1 to 5 and unlock additional buff slots, increase your maximum health (and overall speed!), and bask in bonus upgrades.

  Your bonus score is a global modifier which affects nearly every interaction in the game, from potion potencies to damage output to the overall efficacy of buffs and the length of their cooldowns!
