RED vs BLUE

Defend your teammates and crush your opponents in the Wingdom's thilling 20 vs 20 PvP tournament that pits the Red Team against the Blue Team!


CAPTURE THE FLAGS

Earn critical team bonuses by siezing more territory than your opponent. Territory is defined by 3 flags:

  Team flags begin the game at their respective home base. Steal your opponents' flag to win more territory!

  The neutural white flag is housed at the map's center. Push this flag into enemy space to gain more bonus points!

  Numbered plaques are spread throughout the map, ordered from left to right. Plant flags into them sequentially to advance your territory!


BUFFS & BONUSES

You begin as a slow, hapless wingling, but over time your power will grow. Earn progression bonuses and buffs as you level up and survive wave after perilous wave to rule the Wingdom!

  Yellow potions grant temporory bonus points, while health boosts are awarded by green potions.
